Current Web Design Practices


You will find that this website uses responsive design by way of media queries. This is one modern design feature.

I have also done some looking around other websites selling products and based my image transition on the main index.html page on what other websites seem to do

However, my transitions between those images are not as smooth as others out there! Some even have videos in the background!

I have also read about chatbots being a desirable feature for website designs, to capture customer details and engage customers with the website better.

So, please interact with the chatbot. It is just a free trial, which ends before the assignment is due, but I am pretty sure it will still have functionality, just minimal!

I used the chatbot setup from https://www.tidio.com
